How this Works

This prototype hadles the data from http://104.236.9.88:8080/OncoBlocks/webservice.do?query=get_patient_bundle and make it easy to mine the data. You can see the time line of the patient and explore it's data with relevant sections.

Patient Information:This section shows the information of the selected patients.

Survival Rate:Survival rate has been calculated with respect to exception responders.

Treatment Timeline:All the selected patient indicated on the timeline using chronoline.js

Visulization via D3:Patient has indicated by the D3.js library with respected to the genes. If the indicated genes is present then it is in black color or if it is not present then it is in grey color.

Exceptioal Responders:The set of patient which uniquely respond to the treatment.

Respond Scale of Exceptional Responder (in percentage)The percengae of muted genes is calculated from the set of exceptional resonders which helped in the calculation of survival rate.

Advanced Analytic Section(According to entities)Here you can deeply look through the data of each and every entity to clear the ambiguious situations.
